NYC Cab Open Data Dataset Information

Know the details below to predict fare of your ride

Sl No. Feature Description
1 pickup_datetime The date and time when the meter was engaged for the pickup location
2 dropoff_datetime The date and time when the meter was disengaged for the drop location.
3 passenger_count The number of passengers in the vehicle. This is a driver-entered value.
4 trip_distance The elapsed trip distance in miles reported by the taximeter.
5 pickup_location_id The Taxi Zone in which the taximeter was engaged
6 dropoff_location_id The Taxi Zone in which the taximeter was disengaged.
7 rate_code The final rate code in effect at the end of the trip.
1 = Standard rate
2 = JFK Airport
3 = Newark
4 = Nassau/Westchester
5 = Negotiated fare
6 = Group ride
8 payment_type A numeric code signifying how the passenger paid for the trip.
1 = Credit card
2 = Cash
3 = No charge
4 = Dispute
5 = Unknown
6 = Voided trip
9 fare_amount The time-and-distance fare calculated by the meter.
10 extra Miscellaneous extras and surcharges. Currently, this only includes the $0.50 and $1 rush hour and overnight charges.
11 mta_tax $0.50 MTA tax that is automatically triggered based on the metered rate in use.
12 improvement_surcharge $0.30 improvement surcharge assessed trips at the flag drop. The improvement surcharge began being levied in 2015.
13 tip_amount Tip amount â€“ This field is automatically populated for credit card tips. Cash tips are not included.
14 tolls_amount Total amount of all tolls paid in trip.
15 total_amount The total amount charged to passengers. Does not include cash tips.
